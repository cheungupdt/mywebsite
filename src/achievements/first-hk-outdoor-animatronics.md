# Launched First Outdoor Animatronics for Hong Kong Entertainment

## Overview

Hong Kong, due to it's unique requirement from HKBD, it is tough to have outdoor animatronics. So all the outdoor animatronics has been designed by Imagineers, rather than Entertainment itself. I helped challenged the issue and bring forth the animatronics to life from Entertainment


## Impact

This first ever experience empowered Entertainment and enable a lot of the stuff made by Entertainment possible.
